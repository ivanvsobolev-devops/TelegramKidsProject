# Product Vision

## Product

Telegram Kids

## Mission

Provide a safer Telegram experience for children while preserving access to messaging and communication.

## Core Value Proposition

A child cannot join groups or subscribe to channels without parental approval.

## Target Audience

Parents of children aged approximately 6–14 years.

## Key Principles

- Child safety first.
- Minimal friction for parents.
- Familiar Telegram experience.
- Backend-enforced parental controls.
- Privacy-conscious design.

## Long-Term Vision

Build a family communication platform centered around safe Telegram usage.

Future capabilities:

- Parent mobile application
- Telegram bot for approvals
- Contact approval workflows
- Screen time controls
- Content moderation
- School mode
- Family management
